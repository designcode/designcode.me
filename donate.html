Help Stray Animals

You're probably here because you saw a guy running Amsterdam Marathon with a costume. Good that it caught your eyes as I want to bring awareness to the issue of stray animals in Pakistan.

I grew up in Pakistan and I can tell you, stray animals have a very hard life. They're often abused, neglected, and abandoned. Government often run compaigns to euthanize them in a very inhumane way.

There are a handful non-profit organizations in Pakistan that are doing a great job to help stray animals and you can help them by donating to them. I am listing them below. Instead of taking my word for it, you can check out their instagram profiles and see for yourself.

Warning: Their instagram profiles may contain graphic images as they deal with many animals in need of care.

ACF Animals Rescue

ACF supports the rescue & rehabilitation of the most abused & injured animals in Pakistan. It also creates awareness about the dire need for such a social change that is vital for any civil society.

- Instagram

Strays Home Animal Rescue Foundation

16000+ animals rescued since 2009. Registered Animal Welfare NGO. Providing food, shelter, medical care, and love to the animals in dog, cats and birds.

- Instagram

The Crazy Cats Project

Another non-profit organization that is doing a great job to help stray animals. They are dedicated to providing food, shelter, medical care, and love to the animals in dog, cats and birds.
